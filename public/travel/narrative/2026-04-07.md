```poem
A date on the page and not much else to claim
Just Tuesday and April, politely the same
I stared at the notes and they stared right back
A travel day missing its traveler's track
```

## Tuesday April 7 - The Blank Page

All I had for this day was the heading itself, *Tuesday April 7*, and then nothing. No station names, no meals, no wrong turns, no overheard remarks, not even the usual scraps of logistics that at least prove I had been somewhere.

So this entry stayed blank in the way some travel days do, either because nothing much happened or because too much did and I never got around to pinning it down. By the time I came back to it, there was only the date left, hanging there without explanation.

A day existed. The record did not.
