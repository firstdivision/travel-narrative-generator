## Wednesday April 8 - Early Steps Indoors

I woke early again, mostly because I had gone to sleep early the night before. There was nothing noble about it, just the simple arithmetic of fatigue. Since I was up, I decided this was as good a time as any to find the fitness center in the building and go for a run.

It felt like the sort of small logistical mission that somehow becomes the whole morning when you're in an unfamiliar place, half awake and trying to be functional before the day has really started. Still, it seemed sensible. I was already awake. I had the time. The building, presumably, had a fitness center somewhere. So I went looking for it, with the mild determination that comes from having made a reasonable plan before breakfast.
