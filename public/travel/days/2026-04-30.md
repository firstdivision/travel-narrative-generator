## Thursday, April 30

At work I am treated to lunch by my new work friends.  We go to a restaurant near the work buiding and we order a table of food to be shared what I call "family style" -- in other words -- the plates and bowls of food go in the middle, everyone takes what they want.  There are many dishes, but my favorite was a yellow spicy curry.  I'm not sure what it was called, but some quick Googling says maybe it was "Peranakan-style curry"? In any case I liked it a lot! And I think maybe earned some points for being able to take the heat.

Afterwards my friend and I meet up near where I am staying, and have dinner at a nearby restaurant.  I think this was a variaton of another chicken and rice dish, but when I saw a plate of french fries go buy I could not resist and ordered those too.
