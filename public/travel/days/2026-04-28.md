## Tuesday, April 28

Tonight we head to a mall because I need to buy new luggage to replace my wounded one I have now.

- We find a store that has a lot of options, including Malaysian brands which seems like the appropriate thing to do. The store employees are soon four in number as they bring suitcase after suitcase out to show me the virtures of each one.  "This one has a front zipper", "This one is also very nice".  Eventually I settle on one.  I like the new hardcase style of this carryon suitcase.  

- Dinner is in the mall food court.  Chicken rice and broth.  It is good, but the memory of the Crispy Chicken in the Singapore Airport overshadows it.
